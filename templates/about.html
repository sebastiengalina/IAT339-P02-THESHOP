About us

Our Story

Over 10 years ago, we made a promise to keep floral services easy. Today, we serve over 50 regions in Canada and the U.S. Our selection is above par on options, styles, colours, scents, and we add new breeds every month. Our team is proud to serve you in three languages, and the countless others spoken only through the language of beauty.

Crafting Memories

We specialize in crafting floral fragrances for all occasions, and custom designs suited to your needs. Not only do we design new flower pairings every month, but we make sure that your memories are perfect from petal to package.
